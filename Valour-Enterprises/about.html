About


It started as an ideal in Raleigh, NC. William Krug, CEO of Valour Enterprises was training in medieval combat and the virtues of knighthood resonated deeply and passionately within his bones. William kept those ideals in the back of his head as he progressed through life. Every time a new situation occured, William assed the pros and cons, wondering what he liked, what he didn't, and how it could be made better. One fall morning, an oppurtutity presented itself and William jumped head first into it and hasn't looked back.

Today, Valour Enterprises companies lead their respective industries in customer satisfaction. The thought that a happy employee provides better customer service drives all aspects of the company culture. Monthly cookouts, company sponsored teams, relaxed atomsphere, and engaging work projects have all led to increased employee moral. These happy individuals love what they do and have banded together to form the strongest, most capable team in the area.
